Pancakes Recipe

Description

A pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter.

This recipe comes from the family recipe book of Dakota Kelly (AllRecipes Cook 663357).

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 0.25 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 0.25 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
